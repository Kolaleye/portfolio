ABOUT ME

KEHINDE OLALEYE

Hello, my name is Kehinde Olaleye. I was born in Nigeria, I lived there until I was two years old with my mom and my twin brother. My dad was in the states at this time saving money to bring us over. After about two years of living in Nigera, my dad was finally able to send for us. When we came to America, we lived in a twin story apartment building in San Jose CA. For the next seventeen years, San Jose CA was where I called home. That's where I was raised. Even though I don't live there anymore, I still identify as a San Jose-ian. For as long as I can remember, I'll always wanted to be something in life. I'm not sure what brought that on, but I can remember very vividly the day-dreams I used to have whilst riding in my dad's car when I was young. My twin brother and I also played soccer for the majority of our youth. At one point in time, we were very good at it, but to my father's dismay, we lost passion for the sport. High School was the turning point in my life. It was there where I would set the foundation of my character. Lastly, there is four of us. I also have a little brother and sister.
